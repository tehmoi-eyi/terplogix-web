# TerpLogix HyperFrames Video Identity

## Palette
- Void black: `#030407`
- Obsidian: `#080b12`
- Deep navy: `#0a1730`
- Cyan signal: `#00d7e8`
- Blue signal: `#1977ff`
- Violet signal: `#7f3cff`
- Frost text: `#f4fbff`
- Muted text: `#b7c7d8`

## Typography
- Display: `Aptos Display`, `Segoe UI Variable Display`, `Arial`, sans-serif
- Body: `Aptos`, `Segoe UI Variable Text`, `Arial`, sans-serif
- Data: `Cascadia Mono`, `Consolas`, monospace
- Use very large display type, tight tracking, and strong weight contrast.

## Motion
- Premium, technical, cinematic.
- Use confident entrances, gliding orbit layers, scanner sweeps, and parallax image stacks.
- Avoid playful bounce, chaotic glitch, cannabis cliches, and generic SaaS card grids.

## Composition Rules
- Backgrounds are dark but never flat black.
- Brand accent colors must be visible enough for video compression.
- Screenshots should be treated as product surfaces with depth, tilt, shadow, and motion.
- Copy should be short and declarative.
